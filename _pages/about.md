I am **Dr. Ranabir Chakraborty**, a Soil Scientist at ICAR-Indian Institute of Water Management (ICAR-IIWM), Bhubaneswar.

My research focuses on understanding and improving agricultural systems through **soil science, environmental monitoring, and data-driven modelling approaches**.

---

## Research Areas

My work primarily includes:

- Greenhouse Gas (GHG) emissions in agricultural systems  
- Soil carbon dynamics and nutrient management  
- Remote sensing and GIS applications in agriculture  
- Water resource management and irrigation efficiency  
- SWAT modelling for watershed analysis  
- Sustainable agricultural practices  

---

## Research Approach

I integrate:

- Field experimentation  
- Laboratory analysis  
- Statistical modelling  
- Remote sensing and geospatial techniques  

to generate insights that support sustainable and climate-resilient agriculture.

---

## 🛠️ Tool Development

In addition to research, I am developing web-based tools to make scientific analysis more accessible.

### AgriStat – Agricultural Statistical Tool

AgriStat is designed to simplify statistical analysis in agricultural experiments. It allows users to perform ANOVA, multiple response analysis, and post-hoc tests through an intuitive web interface.
