I am a Soil Scientist at ICAR-IIWM, Bhubaneswar, working on GHG emissions, Remote Sensing, Water management, SWAT modelling, Nutrient dynamics, and Sustainable agriculture.

My research focuses on soil carbon dynamics, nitrogen management using inhibitors, and digital soil mapping using machine learning approaches.

## Featured Tool

**AgriStat – Agricultural Statistical Tool**

AgriStat is a web-based tool developed to support statistical analysis in agricultural experiments. It enables ANOVA (CRD, RBD), multiple response analysis, and post-hoc tests without requiring coding knowledge.
